The Polynomial Method and Restricted Sums of Congruence Classes

Noga Alon, Melvyn B. Nathanson, Imre Z. Ruzsa

Journal of Number Theory 56 (1996), 404-417

We present a general polynomial method for studying restricted sums of congruence classes modulo a prime. The method provides a unified approach to various problems in additive number theory, including the Cauchy-Davenport theorem and the Erd≈ës-Heilbronn conjecture on sums of distinct residues.
